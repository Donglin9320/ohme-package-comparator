Executive read

Three decisions from the matrix.

Start here before opening any detailed competitor table.

Keep 44g trial pouch

Use it for first purchase, campus, office, gym, vending, and convenience-style occasions.

Test 6-pack and 8-pack

Direct freeze-dried competitors and broader snack bundles show repeat buyers compare count, total price, and price per pouch.

Do not copy Bars or chips

Use them only as value and packaging benchmarks. OHME should stay fruit-led, texture-led, and premium.

Survey connection

What this changes in the survey.

The survey should not ask people to solve manufacturing or packaging operations. It should measure shopper behaviour.

1

Recent snack spend

Ask what they bought last time and how much they spent. This is stronger than abstract willingness-to-pay.

2

Direct substitutes

Add freeze-dried fruit brands and an Other text field so new competitors can enter the tracker.

3

Format test

Keep single pouch, 2-pack, 6-pack, and regular pouch. Avoid complicated operational tradeoffs.

4

Message test

Ask which messages make OHME easier to try: real fruit, no added sugar, crunch, made in Canada, bundle value.
